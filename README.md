# Pill Pal

A simple and helpful medication management tool designed to help users track doses, receive reminders, and maintain a clear overview of their daily medication schedule.

##  Features

* **Medication Tracking:** Add, edit, and remove medications.
* **Smart Reminders:** Receive notifications when it's time to take each dose.
* **Clean, Intuitive UI:** Easy for anyone to use.
* **Secure & Private:** Your information stays on your device unless you choose to sync.
